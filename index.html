What is Spiritual Anatomy?

Spiritual anatomy is the study of the energetic structure of the human body. It explores the subtle systems that exist beyond our physical form, including chakras (energy centers) and nadis (energy channels). These invisible pathways influence our physical health, emotional well-being, mental clarity, and spiritual awareness. Understanding spiritual anatomy helps us recognize how energy flows through our body and how we can optimize this flow for better health and consciousness.
