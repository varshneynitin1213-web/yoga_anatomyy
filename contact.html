Frequently Asked Questions

?

Is this website free to use?

Yes, all content on Spiritual Anatomy is completely free. Our mission is to educate and spread awareness about spiritual wellness practices.

?

Can I practice these techniques at home?

Absolutely! All yoga poses and meditation techniques shared here can be practiced at home. Start slowly and listen to your body.

?

How often should I meditate?

We recommend starting with 5-10 minutes daily. Consistency is more important than duration. Gradually increase as you become more comfortable.

?

Do I need any equipment for yoga?

A yoga mat is helpful but not required. Wear comfortable clothing that allows movement. Most poses can be done without any special equipment.
